# Fluid

Framework-agnostic web components, built on Lit. Drop them into React, Vue, Svelte, or plain HTML.

## What's here

- **Components**: `@fluid-ds/components` (Lit-based custom elements, `fluid-*` prefix)
- **Tokens**: `@fluid-ds/tokens` (CSS custom properties: colors, spacing, motion, radii, type)

Browse the sidebar to explore components.
